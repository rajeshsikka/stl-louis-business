St.louis City


The objective was to gather the data related to the number of Businesses categories present in St.Louis City by zip code. The analysis would provide a snap shot to an individual when he/she is checking out a particular area with the intend to live or visit.The business categories are listed below:-
a.) Food (Fast Food Joints, Restaurants)
b.) Stores(Department, Grocery, Gas)
c.) Financial Institutions (Banks, Loans etc.)
d.) Hospitals, Urgent Care Centers
e.) Universities/Colleges, Daycare Centers
f.) Hotels, Bed & Breakfast
