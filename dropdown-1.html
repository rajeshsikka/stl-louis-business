Hotels

Most of the Hotels are in Downtown or nearby surrounding areas.This is due to fact that Busch Stadium (Home of St.louis Cardinals) and Enterprise Center (Home of St.louis Blues) are located in downtown.The St.louis Arch also attracts a number of tourist. A number of good Restaurants are present on Washington Avenue. The Lumiere Casino is also located in downtown. Some of the Hotels listed are as follows:
a. Marriott St. Louis Grand
b. Four Seasons Hotel
c. The Westin St. Louis
d. Hyatt Regency
e. Hilton St. Louis at the Ballpark
f. Embassy Suites St. Louis - Downtown

Colleges and Universities

Most of the Colleges and Universities are in close proximity to Downtown.The most notable
universities (including Medical Colleges) which have a ranking in USA are listed below-
a. Washington University in St.louis
b. Saint Louis University
c. Barnes-Jewish Hospital College
d. Saint Louis University School of Medicine
